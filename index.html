Hello, my name is Miriam.

I am a physicist specialized mostly in quantum optics, numerical simulation, quantum computing and quantum information.
My primary occupation is an R&D position in the industry in a project related to Quantum Key Distribution. There, I am responsible for research and implementation of the classical post-processing QKD phase - error correction with LDPC codes and privacy amplification. I also participate in the design and development of a time-bin based QKD system simulator.

After finishing my PhD, I decided that I want to continue my research more into the direction of classical and quantum (especially quantum) information theory, and I am now trying to evolve in this direction.

Since quantum science right now is mostly a hobby for me, I tend to just look for various problem that catch my interest.
